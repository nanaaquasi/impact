Meet the team

A team of professional researchers, consultants and business executives with solid global strategic alliances and network to deliver with speed and extra-ordinary customer care to our key stakeholders.

Meet the C.E.O

Social Worker, M & E Expert and Researcher

MPhil in Development Studies

Agyenna Kesse-Tachi | CEO

Agyenna, the CEO of Impact Initiatives Limited, is an experienced consultant and researcher with vast experience in business, strategic and marketing planning; M&E and feasibility study spanning seven years. He has successfully prepared tender documents, restructured businesses and helped recapitalize businesses. He was the lead consultant in developing a business plan for the construction of Range 2 Industries, Neuro Surgery Africa, Legon Golf Driving Range/Training Center and Point-Blank Media Concepts. He has undertaken several assignments for EDAIF (now Eximbank), including playing a crucial role that culminated in building Eskay Therapeutic Limited and Lymens Medical Supplies’ ultra-modern pharmaceutical plants. He was the lead consultant in restructuring Life and Leisure Stores Ltd in Nigeria, Geoquaye Company Limited and Edge S and S. Agyenna undertook feasibility study for Concrete Product Limited to establish a block factory and for the FAO’s National Forest Investment Program (NFIP) for the West Africa Forest Convergence Plan.

Other Team Members

Dr.Bernard Nsiah

Senior Consultant - Agribusiness

Dr. Richmond Atta-Ankomah

Senior Consultant – Economist and Statistics

With a research background in economics and industrial development issues, spanning several specific areas including manufacturing, health as well a M&E. Dr. Morgan B. Gordon

Senior Consultant – Finance and Impact Evaluation

Morgan is a motivated strategic thinker and Financial Analyst with extensive, varied and progressive leadership qualities. Kelvin Oduro Nyarko

Mr. Emmanuel Mensah Boakye

Experiences.

  • Bothapraku Company Limited

    September 2015 – October 2015

    Bothapraku Company Limited contracted Impact Initiatives to prepare a five-year business plan to raise US$1 million from strategic investors in Germany to boost its farm operations and export business.

  • Professional Auto Center

    June 2017

    Contracted Impact Initiatives Ltd to do a business proposal for the setting up a break band/pad and clutch disc manufacturing plant, as a subsidiary of Sinter Slovenia Limited. The business plan served the purpose of convincing Sinter Slovenia to set up the subsidiary to Ghana to cover the West African market as well as convince government to invest in the project.

  • EG Holdings

    Jul 2016

    CPL, a subsidiary of EG holdings, contracted Impact Initiatives Ltd to undertake a pre-feasibility studies (and a full feasibility study later) as well as a strategic plan for setting up and operating a block/manufacturing plant in Accra.

  • Perphekt Spring Company Limited

    August 2017

    Perphekt contracted Impact Initiatives Ltd to do a business proposal for the setting up a break band/pad and clutch disc manufacturing plant, as a subsidiary of Sinter Slovenia Limited. The business plan served the purpose of convincing Sinter Slovenia to set up the subsidiary to Ghana to cover the West African market as well as convince government to invest in the project.

  • Neuro Surgery Africa

    December 2015 – February, 2016

    Neuro Surgery Africa is a new neurological hospital facility being set-up by a group of investors in Ghana. Impact Initiatives Ltd was contracted to undertake the business planning and feasibility study for the project.

  • Eskay Therapeutic Company Limited

    July 2014 – August 2015

    Eskay Therapeutic Company Limited is one of the leading pharmaceutical companies in Ghana. In July, 2014, the company contracted Impact Initiatives Ltd to prepare a business plan and other documents to apply for a loan of USD$3,000,000 (GHS 10,800,000) from strategic investors in Ghana and India to set up a new factory.

  • Point Blank Media Concepts Ltd.

    November 2012 – February, 2013

    In 2010, Point Blank Media Concepts Ltd. observed that the product sales system of videos in Ghana and Nigeria was non-existent and hence engaged the services of Impact Initiatives Ltd to prepare a comprehensive business/marketing plan for soliciting USD$7,000,000 and USD$43,038,450.00 to establish the distribution networks in Ghana and Nigeria respectively.

  • Geoquaye Company Limited

    March 2007 – June 2012

    Impact Initiatives Ltd has had a long-term relationship with Geoquaye, starting from 2007. Impact Initiatives has prepared tender documents, business proposals and marketing plans for Geoquaye. In addition, Impact Initiatives has been instrumental in the recruitment of top executives for the company.

  • Others

    Impact Initiatives has also prepared bidding documents, feasibility study, strategic plans and business plans for other small and medium-scale enterprises (SMEs), including Edge S&S (an oil and gas supply company), Arrowhead Investment Limited (a printing house), Opasant Company Limited (a fumigation company) and the Legon Golf Driving Range/Training Center among others.

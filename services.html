Mystery Shopping | Customer Intelligence

Companies spend fortunes advertising to attract new customers to their business but fail to convert these customers into loyal customers who in turn generate several leads for the firm. Indeed, adverts are crucial in attracting new customers but failing to keep new customers is a needless waste of resources.... Our team of researchers provides this advantage by giving you crucial feedback that enables you assess whether your customers are satisfied or not. We use trained observers who pose as customers or potential customers and are hence able to accurately identify the deficiencies in your service delivery processes at all points of contact with the customer and staff.

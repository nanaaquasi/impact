Who We Are

Impact Initiatives Limited is an innovative company providing general advisory services, management, research, training and development consulting services. The company started operations in 22nd May, 2009 with business registration number CA-59,380. ... Over the last seven years, the professionals in Impact has acquired varied experiences in the conduct of surveys, feasibility studies, business planning, strategic planning, business proposals as well as monitoring and evaluation assignment for varied clients. The firm has also been involved in various assignments that involved nationwide collection of data and the analysis thereof to inform policy decisions-making. Our well-resourced team of professional researchers, consultants and business executives have solid global strategic alliances and network to deliver with speed and extra-ordinary customer care, crowning it with a guaranteed satisfaction to our key stakeholders. At Impact Initiative, we value quality output. As such, all our work is quality checked and rechecked before final submission to the client.

Experiences.

  • Bothapraku Company Limited

    September 2015 – October 2015

    Bothapraku Company Limited is leading exporter of organic raw materials to pharmaceutical and cosmetic firms in Europe and the USA. The company has several hectares of farms where it plants its orange and lemon fruits where the peels are dried and exported. The company contracted Impact Initiatives to prepare a five-year business plan to raise US$1 million from strategic investors in Germany to boost its farm operations and export business.

  • Professional Auto Center

    June 2017

    Contracted Impact Initiatives Ltd to do a business proposal for the setting up a break band/pad and clutch disc manufacturing plant, as a subsidiary of Sinter Slovenia Limited. The business plan served the purpose of convincing Sinter Slovenia to set up the subsidiary to Ghana to cover the West African market as well as convince government to invest in the project.

  • EG Holdings

    Jul 2016

    CPL, a subsidiary of EG holdings, contracted Impact Initiatives Ltd to undertake a pre-feasibility studies (and a full feasibility study later) as well as a strategic plan for setting up and operating a block/manufacturing plant in Accra. The assignment involves providing market and operational analysis, macro analysis and financials for the block/manufacturing sector. Impact Initiative was responsible for desk review, scheduling meetings with key stakeholders, conducting interviews with some of the industry players and competitors, conducting market and operational analysis, preparing draft report and final report.

  • Perphekt Spring Company Limited

    August 2017

    contracted Impact Initiatives Ltd to do a business proposal for the setting up a break band/pad and clutch disc manufacturing plant, as a subsidiary of Sinter Slovenia Limited. The business plan served the purpose of convincing Sinter Slovenia to set up the subsidiary to Ghana to cover the West African market as well as convince government to invest in the project.

  • Neuro Surgery Africa

    December 2015 – February, 2016

    Neuro Surgery Africa is a new neurological hospital facility being set-up by a group of investors in Ghana. Impact Initiatives Ltd was contracted to undertake the business planning and feasibility study for the project.

  • Eskay Therapeutic Company Limited

    July 2014 – August 2015

    Eskay Therapeutic Company Limited is one of the leading pharmaceutical companies in Ghana. In July, 2014, the company contracted Impact Initiatives Ltd to prepare a business plan and other documents to apply for a loan of USD$3,000,000 (GHS 10,800,000) from strategic investors in Ghana and India to set up a new factory.

  • Point Blank Media Concepts Ltd.

    November 2012 – February, 2013

    Point Blank Media Concepts Ltd., the parent company of Leisure and Lifestyles Ltd. (L&L), was registered in September of 2003 with its head office in Dansoman, Accra (Ghana). The company has been operating since 2005 as a major producer and distributor of television programmes in Ghana, Nigeria and the rest of Africa. In 2010, Point Blank Media Concepts Ltd. observed that the product sales system of videos in Ghana and Nigeria was non-existent and hence engaged the services of Impact Initiatives Ltd to prepare a comprehensive business/marketing plan for soliciting USD$7,000,000 and USD$43,038,450.00 to establish the distribution networks in Ghana and Nigeria respectively.

  • Geoquaye Company Limited

    March 2007 – June 2012

    Geoquaye Company Limited, as an electrical/mechanical engineering company, has been effectively delivering quality, timely and cost-effective services to its customers in the telecommunication, hospitality, banking and manufacturing industries in Ghana. The company’s core business revolves around the supply, installation and repair of generator sets; the supply, installation, and repair of air conditioners; the assembly, repair, and installation of Intelligent Transfer Switches (ITS)/Automatic Transfer Switches (ATS); Domestic and industrial wiring among others. Impact Initiatives Ltd has had a long-term relationship with Geoquaye, starting from 2007. Impact Initiatives has prepared tender documents, business proposals and marketing plans for Geoquaye. In addition, Impact Initiatives has been instrumental in the recruitment of top executives for the company.

  • Others

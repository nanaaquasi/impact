Who We Are

Impact Initiatives Limited is an innovative company providing general advisory services, management, research, training and development consulting services. The company started operations in 22nd May, 2009 with business registration number CA-59,380. Over the last seven years, the professionals in Impact has acquired varied experiences in the conduct of surveys, feasibility studies, business planning, strategic planning, business proposals as well as monitoring and evaluation assignment for varied clients. The firm has also been involved in various assignments that involved nationwide collection of data and the analysis thereof to inform policy decisions-making. Our well-resourced team of professional researchers, consultants and business executives have solid global strategic alliances and network to deliver with speed and extra-ordinary customer care, crowning it with a guaranteed satisfaction to our key stakeholders. At Impact Initiative, we value quality output. As such, all our work is quality checked and rechecked before final submission to the client.

Feasibility Studies

Our team provides in-depth details about your business to determine if and how it can succeed, and serve as a valuable tool for developing a winning business plan. From our work, you will be guided in your decisions as to the potential benefits or probable losses of taking up a prospective venture before undertaking the actual investing.

  • Business Model to be adopted
  • Technical Study
  • Legal Study

Other Services

Market Research & Due Diligence

Impact Consult has a very strong business development department that attends to all business intelligence, market research and due diligence needs of our clients. Whether it is the introduction of new products or assessing the latent market potentials of an existing product, our team rises up to the challenge and exceed your expectations and offer tailor-made research to make your brand competitive. We are also experienced in database management, automated charting and data delivery useful for large tracking studies or multi region research

Business Plan | Proposal Writing | Preparing Tendering Documents

A well written, straight-to-the-point but comprehensively strategic business plan is always your corporate operational guide; a tool to measure your progress; a compass in your business journey, and also a basis for evaluating the viability of your enterprise. We create a business plan that includes everything a banker, investor or governmental body may require to make an investment decision.

Our business planning services include working with the client to prepare bespoke business plans that ensures all nuanced details of the business have been captured and adequately presented without compromising the simplicity and persuasive nature of the plan/proposal.

Baseline Survey | Monitoring and Evaluation Assignments | Impact Assessment

Impact Initiative is proficient at conducting baseline, mid-line and end-line surveys for all kinds of projects. Our clientele base span across government agencies, bilateral agencies, multilateral agencies and non-governmental agencies. Our M&E ensure that projects are on track and achieving its objectives for being. We firmly believe that a development activity entails change, so a good M&E and impact assessment will show whether the envisaged changes are being realized. Our reports help you assess whether the results are intended or not, direct or indirect, positive or negative, primary or secondary and suggest how to improve the efficiency of implementation, the extent of the desired results achieved and their sustainability.
